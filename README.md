# What is Gas Fueling Visualizer?
The **Gas Fueling Visualizer** is a tool used to simulate a real experience paying for gas-- watching as the gas pump slowly calculated your total. This tool serves to help individuals see how the "small" price of gas (shown on billboards, infront of gas stations, etc.) adds up to make a **larger total than you probably expected**. If individuals have the ability compare the prices of gas and seeing how it truly adds up, they are able to prepare themselves to make the best financial decision possible.

> Side note: Gas that is cheaper than others may not ALWAYS be gas of good quality.
